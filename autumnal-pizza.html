Autumnal Pizza

This pizza is the epitome of fall flavors! It takes advantage of some items offered at Trader Joe's this time of year to create a delicious dinner.

Prep time Cook time Total time
20 minutes 50 minutes 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Trader Joe's Autumnal Pasta Sauce
    • Alternatively use pumpkin puree or just your favorite tomato sauce
  • Premade pizza dough (can be from Trader Joe's or elsewhere)
  • 1/2 sweet onion
  • 1 cup diced butternut squash
  • Around 1 dozen sage leaves
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • All purpose flour (as needed)
  • Shredded mozzarella (to taste)
  • Ricotta (to taste)
  • Prosciutto
  • Black pepper

Directions:

  1. Slice your onion and place in a pan with some oil heated to medium-low heat. Continue to stir occassionally and cook over low heat until your onions are caramelized, about 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees.
  3. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and add in your butternut squash. Boil until fork tender, about 3-5 minutes. Remove from the water.
  4. Place your sage leaves into a small bowl with some olive oil and massage the oil into the leaves. Thinly slice the sage and set aside.
  5. Flour your workspace and shape your pizza dough into your desired shape and size.
  6. Spread some of the Trader Joe's Autumnal Pasta Sauce on your dough, then top with your desired amount of mozzarella.
  7. Top with your caramelized onions, butternut squash, sage, pieces of prosciutto, rosemary, and dollops of ricotta. Add a swirl of olive oil and some freshly cracked black pepper.
  8. Bake your pizza for 10-15 minutes until golden brown.
